The Band

Dastonia is a metal band from Bern, Switzerland, writing original music that fuses heavy, technical riffs with soaring melodic vocals. Influenced by acts like Spiritbox, Bad Omens, Periphery, and Future Palace, Dastonia is carving its own space in the modern metal scene.
